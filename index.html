Google’s Policies

AdSense Vs Google ADX

AdSense
  • Primarily for individual website owners and small publishers
  • Open to a broad range of publishers, easy to join
  • Stable revenue, potentially lower CPM
  • Limited control, automatic selection of ads
  • Basic targeting based on content and user behavior
  • Relies on Google's ad quality standards
  • Primarily uses CPC and CPM models
  • Simple HTML code integration, limited support
Google ADX
  • Geared towards larger publishers with programmatic needs
  • Typically requires higher traffic, more exclusive access
  • Higher revenue potential due to programmatic real-time bidding
  • More granular control over ad inventory and display rules
  • Advanced targeting options, including demographics and retargeting
  • Allows for diverse ad formats, including rich media
